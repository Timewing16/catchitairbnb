Destinations Bookers Love

Sumba Indonesia

Sumba is known as the Forgotten Island of Indonesia. A well-kept secret amongst surfers and adventurers, this hidden gem is, surprisingly, only an hours flight away from the major tourist hub of Bali. Yet it has thus far remained largely untouched by the crush of Eat-Pray-Lovers that inundate its better-known neighbor.

Tainan Taiwan

Tainan is a modern city of commerce and trade, history and culture. It is also a religious center, with more than a thousand temples plus frequent festivals and parades.

Macedonia Greece

The northern region of Macedonia, in Greece, is a fascinating kaleidoscope of landscapes. While the capital, Thessaloniki, has everything to offer from entertainment and culture to shopping and water fun, it is close to the paradise known as Halkidiki, a three-peninsula region where beaches, mountains, and history reign.
